Overview

FeelBright is a digital platform created to help individuals—particularly students and young adults—assess, understand, and improve their emotional intelligence. Our system offers a combination of features, including interactive self-assessment quizzes, personalized feedback based on validated emotional intelligence frameworks, guided emotional wellness exercises, journaling tools, and a library of educational resources. These services are provided through a user-friendly website that adapts to mobile and desktop devices, ensuring accessibility anytime and anywhere with internet connectivity. The platform is designed to promote emotional self-awareness, stress management, empathy, and social skills, contributing to the user’s overall well-being and personal growth. While the platform is built with professional references and psychological principles in mind, it is important to note that FeelBright is an academic project and is not a substitute for professional therapy or mental health services.
